Gurugram, India

Location

Gurugram, often called Gurgaon, is a city located in the northern Indian state of Haryana. It is a part of the National Capital Region (NCR) of India and is situated just southwest of New Delhi, the capital city.

About

Weather

Gurugram experiences a typical North Indian climate with hot summers and cold winters. The monsoon season brings relief from the scorching heat. Winters can be quite chilly, making it a diverse weather experience throughout the year.

Population

Gurugram has a diverse and rapidly growing population, including people from various cultural backgrounds and regions of India. Its cosmopolitan nature is reflected in the mix of languages, traditions, and cuisines you'll find here.

Sports

Gurugram is a hub for sports enthusiasts. It hosts various sports tournaments and has modern sports facilities. Cricket, golf, and soccer are popular sports in the city. Many sports clubs and academies provide opportunities for sports enthusiasts to excel.

Things To Do in Gurugram

  • Visit the Kingdom of Dreams for cultural performances and entertainment.
  • Explore the beautiful Aravalli Bio-Diversity Park and Leisure Valley Park for nature and outdoor activities.
  • Indulge in the diverse and delicious street food culture, including chaats, kebabs, and sweets.
  • Discover the Cyber Hub for a variety of dining and nightlife experiences.
  • Shop at the numerous malls and markets offering everything from high-end brands to local crafts.
