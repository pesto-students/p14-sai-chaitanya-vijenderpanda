A R Rahman Fan Webpage

A R Rahman, also known as A R R, is a legendary Indian musician and composer. He is best known for his work in the Indian film industry, particularly in Bollywood and South Indian cinema. Rahman has received numerous awards, including two Academy Awards, two Grammy Awards, and a BAFTA Award.
